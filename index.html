Multiplayer Dice Game

-------Click the image to check out the project-------

Game with a lot fun based on luck and decissions when to stop and take your points.I explain the rules - 2 players, you have an option to type your username or just enter like guest, after that will be shown button for roll the dice, your current points will be displayed in the section "current", after you press the "roll" button will be shown new one - "hold" button, when you press it all of your current points are collected and moved to your "real score" section.But here have something important, if you roll "1" all current points that you have will be reset to 0 and you losing your turn.So, think twice before you roll the dice. The first player who achieve 100 points (current points + real score points) win the game.Good luck!
